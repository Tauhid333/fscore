If a player scores 3 goals he gets a perfect rating 10.
For each goal a player gets 10 points,for each assists gets 8 points,for each key passes get 4 points,for each interceptions gets 1 point. for each tackles won gets 2 points, for each shots on target gets 2 points.The total points will be divided by 5 to find the final rating.
Max rating is 10.If anyone gets more than 10 his rating will be 10/10.
